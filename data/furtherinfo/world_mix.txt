<h2>Global Energy Mix Time Series</h2>
<p>
<p>
Data on a global, continental, and country was collected and made available from the Our World in Data Website. Specifically, data presented here 
is available at the <a href="https://ourworldindata.org/grapher/per-capita-energy-stacked"  target="_blank"> Our World in Data CO2 Energy Mix data page</a> with a very
detailed discussion and analysis at their <a href="https://ourworldindata.org/energy-mix"  target="_blank"> Our World in Data CO2 Energy Mix</a> page. 
</p>
<p>
Data used by this website was collected from the OWID website above, and then processed using the main.py python script found in the python directory.
</p>
